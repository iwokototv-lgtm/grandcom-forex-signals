## Step 1: Create Google Play Developer Account

1. Go to **https://play.google.com/console/signup**
2. Sign in with your Google account (grandcomfxsignals@gmail.com)
3. Pay the **$25 one-time registration fee**
4. Complete the account details
5. Wait for approval (usually instant, can take up to 48 hours)

## Step 4: Upload to Play Console

1. Go to **https://play.google.com/console**
2. Click **"Create app"**
3. Fill in:
   - App name: `Grandcom Forex Signals Pro`
   - Default language: English
   - App type: App
   - Free or paid: Free (with in-app purchases)
   - Category: Finance

4. Complete the **App content** section:
   - Privacy policy URL
   - App access (no special access needed)
   - Ads declaration (contains no ads)
   - Content rating questionnaire
   - Target audience (18+ for financial apps)
   - News apps (No)
   - COVID-19 apps (No)
   - Data safety form

5. **Store listing**:
   - Upload app icon
   - Upload feature graphic
   - Upload screenshots
   - Add short & full description

6. **App releases**:
   - Go to Production → Create new release
   - Upload your APK file
   - Add release notes: "Initial release"
   - Review and roll out
