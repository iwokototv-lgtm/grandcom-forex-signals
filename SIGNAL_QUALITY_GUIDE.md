> **Service:** serene-growth

## Overview

This guide documents the comprehensive signal quality validation system introduced in v3.1. Every signal generated by the Gold Trading System now passes through 13 independent quality checks before being approved for trading. Signals that fail critical checks are automatically rejected with detailed reasons.

The system addresses 12 known quality issues from v3.0 and adds a 13th dynamic confluence scoring layer.

---

## Quality Issues Fixed

| # | Issue | Old Behaviour | New Behaviour |
|---|-------|--------------|---------------|
| 1 | R:R too low | 1:1.3 accepted | Min 1:2 swing, 1:1.5 scalp |
| 2 | Regime confusion | RANGE vs directional SELL | Strict TREND_UP/DOWN/RANGE/BREAKOUT classification |
| 3 | Entry band too tight | 1-pip zone (unrealistic) | 10-pip zone for live execution |
| 4 | Static confidence | Always 75% | Dynamic from MTF + SMC + momentum + session + news |
| 5 | SL not anchored | Arbitrary SL placement | Anchored to swing high/low |
| 6 | ATR unquantified | No ATR value stated | ATR value + pips + position size derived |
| 7 | Wrong regime entry | Selling at support in RANGE | RANGE SELL must be at resistance (≥70% of range) |
| 8 | Session ignored | Post-NY close not flagged | Session quality flagged (HIGH/MEDIUM/LOW) |
| 9 | Wrong entry position | Entry near day's low for SELL | Entry positioning validated per regime |
| 10 | MTF dropped silently | Confidence stayed static | Confidence recalculated when MTF drops |
| 11 | No signal expiry | Signals never expired | Expiry field: 4h swing, 2h intra, 1h scalp |
| 12 | No news filter | No JOLTS/NFP/FOMC check | High-impact news blocks trading ±30 min |

## 13 Hybrid Indicators

### Indicator 1 — SMC + Order Flow

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `SMCOrderFlowIndicator`

Filters false SMC levels by requiring order flow confirmation. A valid SMC level must have:
- Volume spike ≥ 1.5× average at the level
- Price reaction (rejection candle or engulfing)
- Level not yet mitigated

**Score:** 0–100. STRONG ≥ 75, MEDIUM ≥ 50, WEAK < 50.

---

### Indicator 2 — RSI + MACD + Stochastic RSI

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `TripleMomentumIndicator`

Triple momentum confluence — all three must agree for a HIGH signal.

| Indicator | BUY Signal | SELL Signal |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| RSI | < 40 (recovering from oversold) | > 60 (weakening from overbought) |
| MACD Histogram | > 0 (bullish momentum) | < 0 (bearish momentum) |
| Stochastic RSI | < 40 (oversold) | > 60 (overbought) |

**Score:** 33% per indicator. STRONG = all 3 aligned.

---

### Indicator 3 — VWAP + Price Action

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `VWAPPriceActionIndicator`

Institutional session alignment using VWAP as dynamic support/resistance.

- **BUY:** Price above VWAP + bullish price action (rejection from VWAP)
- **SELL:** Price below VWAP + bearish price action
- **Proximity:** Entry within 0.5 ATR of VWAP = high-quality zone

---

### Indicator 4 — Fibonacci + SMC Confluence

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `FibonacciSMCIndicator`

Stacked zones where Fibonacci retracement aligns with SMC levels.

| Level | Significance |
|-------|-------------|
| 38.2% | Minor retracement |
| 50.0% | Psychological level |
| 61.8% | Golden ratio — OTE start |
| 78.6% | OTE end — highest probability |

**OTE (Optimal Trade Entry):** 61.8%–78.6% retracement zone. Score +50 if in OTE, +30 if Order Block at Fibonacci, +20 if FVG at Fibonacci.

---

### Indicator 5 — ATR + Bollinger Bands

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `ATRBollingerIndicator`

Volatility sizing combined with Bollinger Band squeeze detection.

- **ATR ratio > 1.5:** High volatility — reduce position size 30%
- **ATR ratio < 0.7:** Low volatility — increase position size 20%
- **BB Squeeze:** Width < 80% of average — explosive move imminent
- **BB Position:** Entry at lower band (BUY) or upper band (SELL) = high quality

---

### Indicator 6 — Range + Breakout Filter

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `RangeBreakoutFilter`

Provides regime clarity by distinguishing true breakouts from fakeouts.

| Regime | ADX | Condition |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| RANGE | < 20 | Price oscillating between S/R |
| BREAKOUT | 20–30, rising | ADX rising + volume spike |
| TREND | > 30 | Strong directional move |

**Fakeout filter:** Rejects if price returns inside range within 2 candles after breakout.

---

---

### Indicator 12 — Volatility-Adjusted Position Sizing

**File:** `backend/ml_engine/hybrid_indicators.py` → `VolatilityPositionSizingIndicator`

Sizes positions to risk exactly 1% of account balance.

**Formula:**
```
risk_amount = account_balance × 0.01
sl_pips     = |entry - sl| × pip_multiplier
lots        = risk_amount / (sl_pips × pip_value_per_lot)
```

**Adjustments:**
- High volatility (ATR ratio > 1.5): reduce size by 30%
- Low volatility (ATR ratio < 0.7): increase size by 20%
- News event nearby: reduce size by 50%

**Pip values:**
- XAUUSD (Gold): $1 per pip per lot
- JPY pairs: $9 per pip per lot
- Standard Forex: $10 per pip per lot

## Quality Scoring Methodology

### Dynamic Confidence Formula

```
dynamic_confidence =
    (mtf_score × 0.30)  +   # MTF alignment (30%)
    (smc_score × 0.25)  +   # SMC score (25%)
    (momentum  × 0.20)  +   # Triple momentum (20%)
    (session   × 0.15)  +   # Session quality (15%)
    (news_clear× 0.10)      # News clearance (10%)
```

Where each component is normalised to 0–100.

### Confluence Score Formula

```
confluence_score =
    Σ (check_score × check_weight)
    + bonus_high_confidence (if dynamic_confidence ≥ 80: +5)
    + bonus_full_mtf (if all 3 MTF timeframes aligned: +5)
    - penalty_expired (if signal expired: -30)
    - penalty_news (if news blocking: -25)
```

## Regime Classification Rules

| Regime | ADX | ATR Ratio | MA Slope | Structure | Allowed Sides |
|--------|-----|-----------|----------|-----------|---------------|
| TREND_UP | > 25 | Any | > 0 | HH/HL | BUY only |
| TREND_DOWN | > 25 | Any | < 0 | LH/LL | SELL only |
| RANGE | < 20 | < 1.2 | Flat | Oscillating | BUY at support, SELL at resistance |
| BREAKOUT | 20–30, rising | Any | Any | Breaking level | Either (confirm direction) |
| HIGH_VOL | Any | > 1.5 | Any | Any | Either (reduced size) |
| LOW_VOL | Any | < 0.6 | Any | Any | Mean reversion only |
| CHAOS | Any | > 2.0 | Any | Any | NO TRADING |

### Regime-Specific Entry Rules

**RANGE regime (most important):**
- SELL entries: must be in top 30% of range (near resistance, position_in_range ≥ 0.70)
- BUY entries: must be in bottom 30% of range (near support, position_in_range ≤ 0.30)
- Selling at support or buying at resistance = REJECTED

**TREND_UP regime:**
- Only BUY signals allowed
- Entry on pullbacks (position_in_range ≤ 0.80)
- SELL signals = REJECTED (counter-trend)

**TREND_DOWN regime:**
- Only SELL signals allowed
- Entry on rallies (position_in_range ≥ 0.20)
- BUY signals = REJECTED (counter-trend)

## News Filter Rules

### Blackout Window
- **30 minutes before** high-impact event: trading blocked
- **15 minutes after** high-impact event: trading blocked

### Warning Window
- **60 minutes before** high-impact event: warning flag set
- **30 minutes after** high-impact event: warning flag set

### Gold-Relevant Events (highest priority)

| Event | Impact | Blackout |
|-------|--------|---------|
| NFP (Non-Farm Payrolls) | CRITICAL | ±30 min |
| FOMC Decision | CRITICAL | ±30 min |
| CPI / Core CPI | HIGH | ±30 min |
| Fed Chair Speech | HIGH | ±30 min |
| JOLTS Job Openings | HIGH | ±30 min |
| Beige Book | MEDIUM-HIGH | ±30 min |
| GDP Release | HIGH | ±30 min |
| PPI | MEDIUM | ±30 min |
| ISM Manufacturing | MEDIUM | ±30 min |
| Retail Sales | MEDIUM | ±30 min |

### Currency Relevance for Gold
Events from USD, EUR, XAU, and GBP currencies are considered gold-relevant and trigger the blackout window.

## Signal Expiry Rules

| Trade Type | Expiry Window | Rationale |
|-----------|--------------|-----------|
| SWING | 4 hours | Swing setups remain valid for a session |
| INTRA | 2 hours | Intraday setups expire within half a session |
| SCALP | 1 hour | Scalp setups are time-sensitive |

Expired signals are automatically rejected with reason: `"Signal expired at HH:MM UTC"`.

## R:R Minimum Enforcement

| Trade Type | Minimum R:R | Rationale |
|-----------|------------|-----------|
| SWING | 1:2.0 | Swing trades need room to breathe |
| INTRA | 1:1.8 | Intraday trades need good reward |
| SCALP | 1:1.5 | Scalps have tighter targets |

**Previous behaviour (v3.0):** R:R 1:1.3 was accepted. This is now rejected for all trade types.

## SL Anchoring Rules

### BUY Signals
- SL must be at or below the recent swing low
- Tolerance: up to 1 ATR above swing low (buffer for spread)
- Example: Swing low at 2640.00, ATR = 5.00 → SL must be ≤ 2645.00

### SELL Signals
- SL must be at or above the recent swing high
- Tolerance: up to 1 ATR below swing high
- Example: Swing high at 2660.00, ATR = 5.00 → SL must be ≥ 2655.00

## ATR Quantification

Every signal now includes:
- `atr_value`: Actual ATR value (e.g., 5.23 for XAUUSD)
- `atr_pips`: ATR in pips (e.g., 52.3 pips)
- `sl_pips`: SL distance in pips
- `position_size_lots`: Derived from 1% account risk

## Best Practices

1. **Always provide MTF data** — Dynamic confidence is most accurate with MTF alignment data. Without it, confidence falls back to the static value.

2. **Provide swing high/low** — SL anchoring check requires structure data. Without it, the check is a warning only.

3. **Set trade_type correctly** — SWING, SCALP, or INTRA. This affects R:R minimums and expiry windows.

4. **Include news_events** — Pass the economic calendar events to enable the news filter. Use `EconomicCalendar.is_safe_to_trade()` to fetch events.

5. **Include session_data** — Pass `{"current_time": datetime.utcnow().isoformat()}` for accurate session quality scoring.

6. **Check quality_tier before approving** — Only approve HIGH and MEDIUM tier signals. LOW tier signals should be reviewed carefully.

7. **Monitor quality_summary** — Use the quality summary endpoint to track rejection rates and identify systematic issues.

## Integration with Existing System

The `SignalQualityValidator` integrates with the existing `SignalManager` workflow:

```python

    "symbol":        "XAUUSD",

    "confidence":    75.0,
    "atr":           5.20,
    "account_balance": 10000.0,
    "current_price": 2658.50,
    "swing_high":    2661.50,
    "swing_low":     2638.00,
    "position_in_range": 0.78,
```
